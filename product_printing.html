- 64-inch dye sublimation printer: Create quality textiles and signage, quickly and efficiently

- Faster printing: Print speeds of up to 108.6m²/h
- The complete solution: Epson printer, inks, paper and software designed to work in harmony
- Improved reliability: Minimise head strike and cockling to maximise uptime
